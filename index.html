Cryptocurrency Thing:

Because I needed an API that had a lot of endpoints.

Quick Compare:

Compares the data from the last market day of two Cryptocurrencies.

Pick a Cryptocurrency from each drop down, then click "Compare"

Deep Compare:

Acts like Quick Compare, but with more customization.

To add/remove a new Cryptocurrency, use the "Add" and "Remove" buttons.

Just check which boxes you'd like to include in the comparison.
